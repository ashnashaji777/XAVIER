Welcome to St. Xavier's College Bangalore

St. Xavier's Bangalore is one of the top Business Schools under Bangalore City University. In the unremitting momentum of time, St. Xavier’s College, Bangalore has managed to achieve mainstream prominence. It has become a reconvening point for students from a plethora of backgrounds, dedicated as the College is to be a reagent for the junction of opportunities and strengths through skill development and knowledge-sharing programmes.

The faculty who have proven themselves in their respective fields by providing an excellent blend of rigor and relevance in their teaching; staff who are ever ready to reach out; robust industry interactions; research aimed at solving problems of the real world; a plethora of international collaborative arrangements in the anvil; outreach activities that touch the lives of a wide cross-section of society; and an exemplary track record in career counseling and placement facilitations—all combine to provide a rare synergy that transcends artificial barriers and enables students to follow their hearts with passion and confidence.

MESSAGE FROM DIRECTOR INTERNATIONAL RELATION

Home / Message from Director – International Relation

Dr Anish KM

MBBS, DMRD, FRCR (London)

It's a great pleasure to be The Director of International Relations at St. Xavier’s College, Bangalore, India. My experience and commitment in international relations will enhance the competency of the college as a global player in management education. The college is on a drive for collaboration with various countries in the areas of Academic Exchange, Faculty Resource, Technology Adoption, and Cultural Cooperation to elevate the management program to a global level and create opportunities over time. The students of St. Xavier’s will be given various opportunities to interact and get inspired by global management community resources, faculty, and industry experts. They will have membership in various global professional associations to develop their individual competencies and network as global business citizens. I am always here to inspire the students and staff to explore the opportunities at an international level.

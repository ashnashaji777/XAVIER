OUR PATRON

Home / Our Patron

ST.FRANCIS XAVIER

“Would to God that these men who labor so much in gaining knowledge would give as much thought to the account they must one day give to god of the use they have made of their learning and of the talents entrusted to them” The college is named in honor of St. Francis Xavier, a revolutionary Roman Catholic missionary who pioneered the spreading of Catholicism in India. He is a real testament to what one person can accomplish when filled with the fire of the Holy Spirit, true compassion for the suffering, and a thirst for souls. In his passion for spreading the gospel, in his simple obedience, in his humble disregard for himself, the saint was a near perfect imitation of Christ. An epitome of true devotion and solemn commitment, he is a paragon of altruism to be emulated for the youth of all ages With world-class education and a student-centric approach, we aim to nurture the leaders of tomorrow.

THE DEFINITION OF THE MBA AND PGDM CAN BE PROJECTED

MBA is a 2 Year Master’s Program which is Delivered through a College which is Affiliated to a University and Curriculum will be handled by the University Where as PGDM is a 2 Year Post Graduate Program Which is Delivered through an Autonomous College which is approved By AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education)

ELIGIBILITY

Any Graduate with 50 % Aggregate (45% for SC/ST/OBC). Those who are in Final Sem/ Year also can apply

ADMISSION PROCESS

Admissions are normally offered on the basis of an interview and written test together. Students who have great creative and innovative ideas can include them in the interviews, which could aid for the admission. Good aggregate board scores and entrance results also aid in getting the admission.

FAQ

▶ How Many Specializations can take together in MBA?
Students can take Dual Specializations while doing MBA Program.
▶ What Could be the Average Salary after MBA?
A right Student can get an Average Salary of 5 to 8 Lakh Per Annum and an Outstanding Student can get annual package of 10 to 15 Lakh easily as a fresher.
▶What are the Higher Education Chances after MBA?
Students can join for PhD in Management after completing the MBA from Approved University.
▶ Comparison of MBA with M.Com
MBA is a very vast sector and in that there are lots of courses and streams as well, since it is very vast you can actually get good job opportunities in future and because of that your career will also be good. But if you are opting M.Com, then you have to stick with finance mostly and you will get a job as accountant, accounts manager, assistant account manager, or may financial department head.
▶ Comparison of MBA with CA and ACCA
Today’s competitive world, a post-graduate degree or a professional qualification has become the need of the hour to widen the career scope. MBA is quick, glamorous and performers get faster recognition. But acquiring a CA takes a lot of plodding and perseverance; at the end of the day, a result may prove inconclusive. Job profiles would be completely distinct.
Starting as a CA, you can expect to climb up the ladder up to the CFO position. While an MBA’s career path could lead up to CEO MBAs are exposed to all aspects of the business, but CA is not
MBAs score when it comes to communication skills, presentation and customer orientation. The ‘soft skills’ so crucial for success in today’s corporate world Jobs in the CA domain: Statutory & Regulatory compliance, Consolidations, Taxation, Global Accounting (GAAP), Internal Audit, Systems Audit, Transaction Advisory

▶ I have not received my 6th semester marks card yet, how can I apply for the MBA program?
Yes. You can apply. But you will be provisionally admitted, subject to the production of provisional certificate/consolidated mark sheet as proof of fulfilling the prescribed eligibility criteria before the prescribed date, failing which provisional admission granted, will be cancelled.
▶ Can I change my specialization during my course of study?
No, change of course will not be entertained in any case. Candidates seeking admission to the various courses are requested to choose their course of study with utmost care.

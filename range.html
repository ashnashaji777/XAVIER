RANGE & OPPURTUNITIES

Home / Range & Oppurtunities

Job Shadowing

Job shadowing is a career exploration activity that offers an opportunity for a student to spend time with a professional currently working in their career field of interest. Job shadowing offers a chance to see what it’s actually like working in a specific job. Not only do students get to observe the day-to-day activities of someone in the current workforce but they also have an opportunity to gain valuable inside knowledge about a profession that would be difficult to receive otherwise.


Externship & Internship

Externships are live projects which students undertake along with their studies. These projects run parallel to academic schedules and students are expected to find time to work on the live projects after classroom sessions and during the weekends. Internships are full time opportunities which aid in on-the-job learning. Externships and Internships are integral to SXB Bangalore academic pedagogy and prepare students for better placement opportunities , the international hub for businesses and corporate enterprises, gives a very wide range of opportunities for Externships and Internships. This gives a specific advantage to the students to build their industry readiness for superior placement.

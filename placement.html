Placements

There are many opportunities available for graduates, especially in the IT hub of India, Bangalore. St Xavier’s, one among the top business schools in this city, has maintained an excellent track of placement statistics in the past years. After their BBA, MBA or PGDM courses along with dedicated mentorship from our faculty, our graduates land themselves in their perfect dream jobs with high remuneration.

At St Xavier’s, we have a well-efficient placement assistance cell that offers continuous guidance for every individual student, to know their aspirations, their fields of interest, and their dream job, and mentor them holistically whenever needed.

Primary Objectives:

  • Bridge any gaps between the college and industry
  • Offer one-one mentorships
  • Strengthen the industry relations for overall growth
  • Organize career-aiding activities such as industrial visits, workshops, conferences from global industry leaders
  • Help students to master the industry-specific skills
  • Aims to place most of our graduates at top-reputed firms through in-campus, off-campus or other campus fairs

TRAINING CELL

This is the platform where all the crucial shaping and molding of an individual into a graduate of prime quality takes place.

  • Personality Development Program
  • Communication Skills Program
  • Develop technical skills such as Microsoft Excel, Social Media Management, and Digital Marketing.
  • Resume building classes.
  • Preparation for industrial interviews, psychometric tests, and assessment centers.
  • Industry to Institute Programs – Corporate influential from leading industries are invited regularly for interactive sessions with students.
  • Various Training Programs are organized to train our students in the areas of Aptitude, Quantitative Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, and Verbal through industrial experts.
  • Developing communication skills and foreign language skills.
  • Training through mock interviews for students to perform well in the interview rounds as per corporate expectations.
  • Opportunities for various internships, part-time jobs, and CA Articleships.

Primary Objectives:

Along with the aforementioned measures, the professional counselling experts at our placement cell ensure students are offered the right career guidance and they make sure students are always abreast of the modern industry trends which help in getting suitable jobs.

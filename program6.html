PROGRAM OVERVIEW

This course aims to provide hands on experience on networking at an academic level. Here each and every subject is selected are especially for the students to know the real meaning of business. The pedagogy offers theoretical as well as practical knowledge so the students can gain maximum expertise an unleash maximum business potential. Corporate BBA consists of subjects like economics, accounting, marketing, managements, etc. In the first phase, the course strikes to give an international exposure to students with the industry and the changing economy. The second phase is all about imparting practical knowledge with application-based learning to the students to maximise their potential.

Why BBA Global at St. Xavier's?

Corporate BBA (Bachelors in Business Administration) is an undergraduate program in commerce which entails the study of the specifics of management, corporate law, corporate structure, financial management, business economics, corporate government, marketing etc. St. Xavier’s Bangalore, with its fine pedagogy, highly trained faculty, excellent infrastructure and modern teaching methods is a very good choice for any aspiring business student. Corporate BBA programs cover the basics of management studies and equip a student with the requisite quantitative, verbal and logical reasoning skills that will be essential to clear the management entrance examinations. The pedagogy is based on current industry requirements, hence, is more practical and experience-oriented when compared to other colleges. The college emphasises the development of interpersonal and communication skills and demands consistently impressive performance in all core subject areas. Corporate BBA lays the groundwork for MBA and the advantages St Xavier’s gives prepares its students for future educational endeavours. The course is AICTE accredited, which is a guarantee of the quality of education it provides. The placement cell of the college takes great care to ensure that the student’s preparation meets the industry standards. Workshops, conferences, and industry visits are conducted to improve industry readiness. The students are provided with training to ace psychometric tests and professional examinations. Personal counselling sessions are conducted to assess and improve the specific deficits of each student. A litany of internship opportunities is provided along with foreign language training to prepare students for workplaces outside the country. Corporate BBA at St Xavier’s is a great choice for any highly motivated and studious student

DURATION

This is a 3 year full-time course.

ELIGIBILITY

Pass with 45% aggregate for 12th Standard Examinations (10+2) from any stream, preferably from Commerce stream.

ADMISSION PROCESS

Admissions are normally offered on the basis of an interview and written test together. Students who have great creative and innovative ideas can include them in the interviews, which could aid for the admission. Good aggregate board scores and entrance results also aid in getting the admission.

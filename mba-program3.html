PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is a prestigious and comprehensive graduate-level degree that provides individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the field of business and management. The MBA core curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in key business disciplines and equip students with a well-rounded understanding of various aspects of business administration.The MBA core courses typically cover a wide range of subjects that are essential for success in today’s dynamic business environment. These courses aim to develop students’ analytical, critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities. The core curriculum generally includes courses in the following areas: Core courses like Accounting and Financial Management,Marketing Management,Organizational Behaviour,Operations Management,Strategic Management, provide a solid foundation in key business disciplines and prepare students for advanced electives and different specialization courses .St. Xavier’s Bangalore is renowned for its MBA program, which offers a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum. The core courses offered by St. Xavier’s Bangalore cover all the key areas mentioned above, providing students with a well-rounded understanding of business administration. The program emphasizes practical application and real-world problem-solving, preparing students to take on leadership roles in various industries. The program also focuses on developing students’ communication, teamwork, and leadership skills through interactive learning methods and opportunities for personal and professional development. In addition to the core courses, St. Xavier’s Bangalore offers a range of elective courses and specialization options, allowing students to tailor their MBA experience to their specific interests and career goals. The program also provides networking opportunities, industry interactions, and career services to help students build connections and enhance their employability.

DURATION

2 Year fulltime programme

ELIGIBILITY

Aggregate 50% marks (45% for reserved category) in Graduation from any Recognized University.
Candidates must appear any of the entrance exam like XAT/CAT/MAT/C-MAT/N-MAT/K-MAT/G-MAT/K-MAT/PGCET

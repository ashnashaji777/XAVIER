Admission Reservation Cost for UG and PG program is 25000 and 50000 respectively Candidate once reserved the seat cannot be cancelled unless there is a valid reason. In case of discontinuation of course under any circumstances,the student will have to pay complete fee. Payment of fee should be made on or before the prescribed dates notified by the authority, failing which admission will be fortified. If any candidate is applying for any change of course has to happen in one month of class commencement. Other than that will not be entertained.. Admission will be made only after completion of Screening. Each candidate will get the notification from concerned department about the interview date and time. All the Academic Credentials should be submit as and when it notified.

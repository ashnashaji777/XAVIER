PROGRAM OVERVIEW

B.com Corporate is an undergraduate degree program that deals with the study of the executive to assist directors in these efforts, helping the board and providing information on the practices of other companies. Certified institutions like us providing job opportunities to the graduated aspirants for the post of financial analyst, tax consultant, business consultant etc. Policy making, financial agencies, banking sector, development sector etc are the various recruitment areas after pursuing the B.com Corporate Course. The B.com Corporate program involves study concepts of Corporate Governance with specific focus on the areas of company law, secretarial practice and institutional training for the students in public sector as well as private companies.

Why B.Com at St. Xavier's?

St. Xavier’s is a premier institute with an excellent academic track record. The learning environment of the institute is favourable and conducive to intellectual development and the acquisition of skills. The institute is situated in Bangalore which is a world-renowned IT hub. It is also one of the safest cities in the country with a high literacy rate, excellent infrastructure and warm people. The educational infrastructure of the college is good with well-equipped classrooms, wireless internet service, and teaching aids such as multimedia projectors.

Throughout the academic year, myriad guest lectures and seminars are conducted to help inculcate an entrepreneurial spirit and mindset in students. B.Com or Bachelor of Commerce is a three-year degree that encompasses the length and breadth of commercial disciplines and sub-disciplines. It aims to provide knowledge of various aspects of management, finance, accounting and business analytics. This is in addition to data interpretation and logical reasoning, verbal skills and reading comprehension, and quantitative reasoning skills.

St. Xavier’s new age pedagogy emphasizes industry readiness through practical application-oriented teaching helps the students stay relevant in the market. The curriculum is comprehensive and rigorous and is supplemented with workshops and conferences. Oral and written communication skills are improved systematically throughout the course. Various internship opportunities are available for those who are graduating and internships at St Xavier’s Bangalore are longer than those in other colleges. Therefore, industry exposure is significant. The B.Com course is a sound base for higher studies and St. Xavier’s provides an excellent environment to acquire the degree. The placement cell ensures the B.Com final year students get the right internships and are challenged with mock interviews, psychometric test preparations and foreign language training. The faculty are highly credentialed and experienced. They keep up to date with developments in the industry, focus on the weakness of each student and provide them with the required guidance in and out of the curriculum. St. Xavier’s Bangalore is a great choice for acquiring a B.Com degree, for any student seeking a career in commerce.

DURATION

This is a 3 year full-time course.

ELIGIBILITY

Pass with 45% aggregate for 12th Standard Examinations (10+2) from any stream, preferably from Commerce stream.

ADMISSION PROCESS

Admissions are normally offered on the basis of an interview and written test together. Students who have great creative and innovative ideas can include them in the interviews, which could aid for the admission. Good aggregate board scores and entrance results also aid in getting the admission.
